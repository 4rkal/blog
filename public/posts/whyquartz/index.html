Why I switched to Quartz from Hugo

A little bit of backgroundÂ¶

I published my first post on my medium blog in December 2021. I was publishing a couple of post per month and finally joined the glorious medium partner program.

For those who don’t know what the partner program is, it’s a way to monetize your content by paywalling it behind a medium subscription . You can only read a couple of free posts per month. This means that the majority of my audience could not read my posts.

So I decided to launch my own blog. After some research I found hugo. Hugo is quite similar to quartz since you write the content in markdown. It is also written in my favorite programming language (at the moment) [[golang]] .

Hugo is a great tool, however since early 2024 I started writing all my new blog posts and my personal notes in [[obsidian]].
